Our Story

Historians call Coffeehouses the headquarters of the American Revolution. This is where Americans went to talk politics and learn from their leaders. Coffeehouses played a large role in shaping the nation's political landscape. In 2023, Coffeehouse was founded with the goal of revolutionizing the political industry by creating a new channel connecting voters with their candidates. We believe civic apathy from the people of America has led to the DNC, the RNC, corporations, and the bureaucracies of America to ...subvert the power of the people and to usurp for themselves the reins of government... - George Washington. We are proud Americans who are fighting to put the power of government back in the people's hands. Coffeehouse will be the digital manifestation of the 18th century Coffeehouse.
