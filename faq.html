We are located in downtown La Crosse at 511 Main Street! See Home page for map.

How Do I Sign Up?

You can sign up in person at the gym, through text, or email.

What Do I Need to Bring for My First Class?

Gym shorts, t-shirt, and a water bottle are all you need. There is no obligation to buy any gear.

What Age Can My Child Start Jiu Jitsu?

The simple answer is the earlier the better, we have programs for children of all ages.
