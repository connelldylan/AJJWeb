Coffeehouse

The Platform For Democracy

Why spend hours researching your candidates? Get quick and transparent information about your candidates from the local level to the federal level. With over 1.9 billion democratic citizens in the world, most of whom own a smartphone, Coffeehouse aims to become the platform for democracy. A place where citizens can go to complete any civic action.

Interactive Design Prototype

Product Flow

  1. When voters sign up, they will input their address. Based on their address, Coffeehouse will show the user their ballots for upcoming elections. Each ballot shows the polling place address as well as the election date.
  2. When a user clicks on a ballot, they will see all of their candidates organized by office.
  3. A user clicks on a candidate's profile picture to research their candidates.
  4. If a candidate is their favorite candidate, they click the button on the candidate's profile picture which will update the ballot screen with a sticker next to the user's favorite candidate. Once a user has a sticker next to their favorite candidate for every office, they have a fully researched ballot.
  5. They can then bring their phone to their polling place and vote based on the Coffeehouse app if the precinct allows that. If not, the user can write down their candidates on a piece of paper, post-it, index-card, etc.

Important Notes:

  • Click on Joe Biden to see what a profile will look like of a candidate who hasn't signed up for the platform.
  • Click on Donald Trump to see what a profile will look like of a candidate who has signed up for the platform.
  • Click the first ballot square to see what a plurality voting ballot will look like.
  • Click the second ballot square to see what a ranked choice voting ballot will look like.
