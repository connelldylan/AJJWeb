The Platform For Democracy

HUGE Problems in the Political Industry:

  1. Voter turnout rates are so low because there is no easy way to research candidates.
  2. No one runs for political office because it's so hard to win without significant capital.
  3. The Democrat and Republican parties control the channels that connect candidates with voters, effectively disallowing independents and third party candidates from winning.
  4. Americans are conducting their national political discourse on global social media platforms that reward echo chambers.

Our Mission:


Coffeehouse aims to become the platform for democracy, a place that makes it simple for the over 1.9 billion democratic citizens in our world to be civically active.

Our Core Values:

  3. Democratic
  4. Impactful
